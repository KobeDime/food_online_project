# Kobe Pinoy Plates - Online Food Delivery System

Welcome to Kobe Pinoy Plates, a dynamic online food delivery platform that brings the best restaurants to your fingertips. Whether you're in the mood for a local delicacy or an international dish, our system allows you to explore, order, and enjoy a wide variety of meals from the comfort of your home. The system also features a robust admin panel for efficient management of orders, restaurants, and more.

## Key Features

### 1. Restaurant and Menu Selection
- **Browse Restaurants:** Discover a diverse selection of restaurants, each offering a unique menu.
- **Menu Exploration:** View detailed menus and choose your favorite dishes from your preferred restaurant.

### 2. Cart & Order Process
- **Add to Cart:** Select dishes and add them to your cart with ease.
- **Order Confirmation:** Confirm your order and proceed to payment.

### 3. Secure Payment Options
- **Multiple Payment Methods:** We offer secure payment options including:
  - PayPal
  - MasterCard
  - Maestro
  - Stripe
  - Bitcoin

### 4. Admin Panel
- **Manage Orders:** Easily track and manage all incoming orders.
- **Restaurant & Menu Management:** Update restaurant details, manage menus, and more through an intuitive admin interface.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript
- **Backend:** PHP
- **Database:** MySQL
